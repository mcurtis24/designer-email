## üìß Email Templates

AI can generate **7 different types of emails**, each optimized for its purpose:

### Newsletter
**Best for:** Regular updates, school news, community bulletins
**What AI creates:** Welcome section, multiple content sections with headings, images, call-to-action buttons
**Example:** "Create a weekly newsletter with upcoming field trip info, picture day reminder, and sports team celebration"

### Event Announcement
**Best for:** Fundraisers, performances, meetings, open houses
**What AI creates:** Event details (what, when, where, who), images, RSVP button, contact info
**Example:** "Announce spring concert on April 15th at 6pm in the auditorium, featuring choir and band"

### Important Announcement
**Best for:** Policy updates, new initiatives, general information
**What AI creates:** Clear heading, structured information, supporting visuals, next steps
**Example:** "Share new drop-off and pick-up procedures starting next Monday"

### Reminder
**Best for:** Gentle nudges about deadlines, forms, or actions
**What AI creates:** Friendly tone, clear deadline, action button, help contact
**Example:** "Remind parents that field trip permission slips are due by Friday"

### Emergency/Urgent
**Best for:** School closures, safety alerts, time-sensitive information
**What AI creates:** Urgent indicator, essential info only, clear action steps, timeline
**Example:** "School closure tomorrow due to severe weather, updates at 6am"

### Promotion
**Best for:** Fundraisers, merchandise sales, special offers
**What AI creates:** Compelling headline, benefits, promotional image, offer details, strong call-to-action
**Example:** "Promote spirit wear sale - 20% off all items through Friday"

### Welcome
**Best for:** New students, new families, new staff, orientation
**What AI creates:** Warm greeting, what to expect, key resources, getting started button
**Example:** "Welcome new kindergarten families to our school"

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "AI button not working"
- Check that API key is in `.env` file
- Restart the dev server
- Verify key is valid at console.anthropic.com

### "Generation failed"
- Check internet connection
- Verify budget not exceeded
- Try simpler prompt
- Check error message for details

### "Results not what I expected"
- Be more specific in prompt
- Choose different template type
- Adjust audience or tone settings
- Try generating again with refined prompt

### "Budget exceeded"
- Wait until midnight for auto-reset
- Or increase daily budget in `.env`
- Contact admin to adjust limits
